Business Survivors Club – SME Connect is a dynamic hub uniting small businesses across Zambia. Our immersive bootcamps, cutting-edge online tools and networking events help entrepreneurs learn, pitch, connect and grow.

Our Programs

Bootcamps & Training

Intensive province-level workshops in Lusaka, Copperbelt, Southern and other regions. These multi-day trainings cover business fundamentals, leadership and sector-specific skills (agribusiness, manufacturing, creative industries, digital services). Learning is reinforced with micro-courses and a gamified system of badges and challenges, turning raw ideas into strategic businesses.

Mentorship & Coaching

Ongoing coaching connects each entrepreneur with experienced mentors. We host speed-mentoring tables at events and coordinate one-on-one guidance via our online portal. Alumni and experts serve as Ambassadors who volunteer to advise new members, ensuring continuous personal support beyond the bootcamps.

Innovation Showcases

An annual 14-week Business Survivors Competition plus year-round opportunities for pitching. Entrepreneurs enter with new ideas, receive mentoring and pitch training, and face staged challenges. Top finalists gain investor preparation and exposure. A permanent Digital Pitch Marketplace on our platform lets SMEs display products and funding proposals to registered investors at any time.

Technology Platform

  • E-Learning Suite: On-demand courses and micro-modules
  • SME Directory: Searchable business profiles and testimonials
  • Marketplace & Pitch Board: Virtual storefronts and investor pitches
  • AI Tools: Analytics for market research, financial planning, marketing
  • Community Forum & Impact Dashboard

Networking Events & Awards

Regular high-profile summits, expos, roundtables and meetups bring SMEs together with government, investors and corporates. The flagship Business Survivors Summit and SME Expo spotlight innovation and policy discussions. Thematic Master Circles (on ESG, digital trade, youth and women's entrepreneurship, etc.) run alongside. Quarterly stakeholder roundtables and local city meetups ("Business Survivors Connect") sustain momentum. We celebrate success through the annual SME Transformation Awards and quarterly Spotlight Stories for standout entrepreneurs. These initiatives raise public awareness of SME achievements and affirm that "no SME or stakeholder can afford to operate in isolation".

How the SMS Campaign Works

  1. Text JOIN to +260XXXXXXXX to start registration by SMS.
  2. Follow the prompts: reply with your name, province, and membership tier.
  3. Receive confirmation and start getting event updates, mentorship check-ins, and polls by SMS in your preferred language.

About

Business Survivors Club – SME Connect is not just another business network—it's Zambia's national engine for SME transformation. We unite entrepreneurs, innovators, and partners in a vibrant digital-physical ecosystem that breaks down barriers and accelerates growth for every small business, everywhere in Zambia.

  • Vision: To be the heartbeat of Zambia's SME revolution—a platform where every entrepreneur, from Lusaka to the most remote province, can learn, connect, and thrive. We envision a future where Zambian SMEs are globally competitive, digitally empowered, and celebrated as the backbone of our economy.
  • Mission: Deliver a seamless, all-in-one hub that fuses immersive bootcamps, AI-powered e-learning, a live business directory, and a digital pitch marketplace. Our programs are designed to unlock opportunity: from hands-on mentorship and innovation showcases to real-time analytics and investor matchmaking. Every member gains the tools, networks, and recognition needed to turn ambition into impact.
  • What Sets Us Apart: SME Connect is built for resilience and scale. We partner with government, corporates, and global experts to ensure every initiative is data-driven and future-ready. Our gamified learning, continuous feedback, and public recognition programs create a culture where SMEs don't just survive—they lead Zambia's economic transformation. Join us and be part of a movement that's shaping the future of business in Zambia.
